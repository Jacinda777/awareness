Security Tips

We are a community-driven initiative focused on educating individuals and businesses about cybersecurity threats and best practices.

Password Security

  1. Use complex passwords with upper and lowercase letters, numbers, and symbols.
  2. Avoid common words and patterns.
  3. Use unique passwords for each account.

Secured Wifi

  1. Avoid public Wi-Fi or use a VPN.
  2. Secure home networks with strong passwords and WPA3 encryption.
  3. Keep router firmware updated.

Social Engineering

  1. Be wary of unsolicited requests for sensitive information.
  2. Verify identities before sharing details.
  3. Avoid oversharing on social media.
